# Sample corpus: a tiny tech company

Acme Robotics is a company founded in 2019 by Dana Ruiz and Sam Okafor.
It builds warehouse robots. Its flagship product is the Pallet Pilot, an
autonomous forklift.

Dana Ruiz previously worked at Globex, where she led the navigation team.
Sam Okafor studied control theory at Riverside University and worked for two
years at Initech on motion planning.

The Pallet Pilot uses a LiDAR sensor and a planning module called RouteMind.
RouteMind was developed in partnership with Riverside University. In 2023,
Acme Robotics acquired a startup named GridSense to improve its mapping.

Competitors include Globex, which sells the HeavyHauler robot, and a newer
company, BluePeak, whose product is the SwiftLift. BluePeak was founded by a
former Initech engineer.
